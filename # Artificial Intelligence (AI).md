# Artificial Intelligence (AI)

## Introduction
Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a technology that allows machines and computers to think, learn, and make decisions like humans. It helps in solving problems, understanding data, and performing tasks automatically.

## What is Artificial Intelligence?
Artificial Intelligence is the ability of a machine to mimic human intelligence. It learns from data, identifies patterns, and makes decisions based on that learning.

## Examples of AI
- Voice assistants like Siri and Google Assistant  
- YouTube and Netflix recommendations  
- Google Maps navigation  
- Face recognition in mobile phones  
- Chatbots like ChatGPT  

## How AI Works
AI works by using data and algorithms:
1. Data is collected  
2. The system learns patterns from the data  
3. It makes predictions or decisions based on learning  

## Types of AI
### 1. Narrow AI
- Designed for a specific task  
- Example: Voice assistants, recommendation systems  

### 2. General AI
- Can perform any intellectual task like humans  
- Still under development  

## Advantages of AI
- Saves time and effort  
- Reduces human errors  
- Works faster than humans  
- Helps in medical and scientific research  

## Disadvantages of AI
- Can reduce job opportunities  
- Expensive to develop  
- Depends heavily on data  
- Lack of human emotions  

## Applications of AI
- Healthcare (disease detection)  
- Education (smart learning apps)  
- Transportation (self-driving cars)  
- Business (customer support chatbots)  

## Conclusion
Artificial Intelligence is transforming the world by making machines smarter and more efficient. It is an important technology that will continue to grow and impact our daily lives.

## One-Line Definition
Artificial Intelligence is the ability of machines to learn from data and perform tasks that normally require human intelligence.
