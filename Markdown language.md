### # Artificial Intelligence (AI): A Complete Guide

## What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines. These machines are designed to think, learn, and make decisions like humans.Artificial Intelligence, or AI, is a technology that allows machines to think and act like humans. It is becoming a part of our daily life, even if we don’t always notice it. AI is a field where computers are designed to perform tasks that usually require human intelligence, such as learning, thinking, and decision-making. There are different types of AI, like narrow AI which works for specific tasks, and general AI which can perform tasks like humans but is still under development.


## Types of AI

### 1. Narrow AI (Weak AI)
- Designed to perform specific tasks
- Examples: Voice assistants, recommendation systems

### 2. General AI (Strong AI)
- Can perform any intellectual task a human can do
- Still under research and not yet achieved

### 3. Superintelligent AI
- Surpasses human intelligence
- Mostly theoretical and futuristic

---

## Key Technologies Behind AI

- **Machine Learning (ML)** – Systems learn from data
- **Natural Language Processing (NLP)** – Helps machines understand human language
- **Computer Vision** – Enables machines to interpret images and videos
- **Robotics** – Combines AI with physical machines

- Handles repetitive tasks efficiently
- Processes large amounts of data quickly
